# 3 Meditation Exercises
Meditation can help calm your mind and reduce feelings of depression. Here are three exercises you can try:

1. **Body Scan Meditation**
	* Duration: 10 minutes
	* Instructions: Lie down or sit comfortably, and bring your attention to different parts of your body, starting from your toes and moving up to the top of your head. Notice any sensations, feelings, or thoughts without judgment.
	* Why it helps: This exercise helps you develop mindfulness and releases physical tension.
2. **Loving-Kindness Meditation**
	* Duration: 10 minutes
	* Instructions: Sit comfortably, and focus on sending kind thoughts to yourself and others. Repeat phrases like "May I be happy, may I be healthy, may I be at peace" and extend them to others, including friends, family, and even those you may have difficulties with.
	* Why it helps: This exercise cultivates self-compassion, empathy, and understanding.
3. **Mindful Breathing Meditation**
	* Duration: 5 minutes
	* Instructions: Sit comfortably, and focus on your breath, noticing the sensation of the air entering and leaving your nostrils. When your mind wanders, gently bring it back to your breath without judgment.
	* Why it helps: This exercise helps calm your mind, reduces stress, and improves concentration.
